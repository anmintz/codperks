Call of Duty: World at War - Zombie Mode Perk Guide

A 2008 World War II video game which features a bonus zombie survival mode. Based off the perks found in the regular Multiplayer player versus player mode, in the zombie mode they take the form of purchasable "perk sodas" that give the player extra benefits to help them survive.

Perk-a-Colas

Juggernog



Makes the player stronger.
  • Cost: 2,500
  • Increases health by 150%
  • As a result, the player requires 5 hits to be killed rather than 2

Double Tap Root Beer



Makes the player's weapons shoot quicker.
  • Cost: 2,000
  • Increases fire rate by 33%
  • As a result, time to kill for all weapons is quicker

Speed Cola



Makes various player actions faster.
  • Cost: 3,000
  • Reduces weapon reload time by 50%
  • Reduces barrier repair time

Quick Revive Soda



Makes the player revive teammates faster.
  • Cost: 1,500
  • Reduces revive time by 50%
  • In solo, this perk has no use


Tier List
